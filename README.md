# nakdong-chla-drought-dynamics
WRR Data repository: Intra-annual chlorophyll-a dynamics under hydroclimatic extremes in Nakdong River Basin (2010-2022)

Nakdong river water quality and drought indices dataset (2010-2022)
Water quality file: 'water_quality_nakdong_2010_2022.csv'

Hydromet file: 'hydromet_nakdong_2010_2022.csv'

Drought indices file: 'drought_indices_(SPI,SPEI)_nakdong_2010_2022.csv';  'drought_indices_(SSI)_nakdong_2010_2022.csv';'drought_indices_(EDDI,SPEDI)_nakdong_2010_2022.csv';
Temporal: 13 years daily observations (2010-2022); Spatial: 22 sub-basin of Nakdong river basin (as B1 to B22)


Sources of data: 
Water quality data from Korea Ministry of Environment, National Institute of Environmental Research (NIER) (https://water.nier.go.kr/); Hydrometeorological data (P, T) from Korean Water Resources Management Information System (https://www.wamis.go.kr/)
